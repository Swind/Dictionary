test

/test/ n [C]

Definition

› a set of questions or practical activities that show what someone knows or what someone or something can do or is like:
a spelling test
› A test is also a medical examination of part of your body or of something from your body:
an eye test
a DNA test
› A test is also an act of using something to find out whether it is working correctly or how effective it is:
a safety test
